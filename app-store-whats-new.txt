Introducing Tempo64 Studio. Plus a big upgrade for everyone.

TEMPO64 STUDIO (new)
A subscription tier that unlocks unlimited voice and face-cam commentary, watermark-free exports, and a gold Studio app icon for your home screen. Available monthly or yearly — cancel anytime in Settings.

FACE-CAM COMMENTARY (Studio)
Record up to 30 seconds of yourself talking about any move. Your video composites as a circular bubble over the board, with a thought-bubble trail pointing at the move under discussion and a synced waveform showing how long the talk runs. Same hold-to-record + drag-to-lock gesture as voice commentary, with haptic feedback at every step.

VOICE COMMENTARY (Studio)
Record narration on any move; the export splices it in with a synced animated waveform card. Free tier includes one voice clip and one face-cam clip per game so you can try before subscribing.

ANIMATED PIECE MOVES
Pieces now slide from square to square instead of cutting instantly. The capture, check, and checkmate SFX land when the piece arrives at its destination — even at fast pace, you can track every move.

QUIET BEAT AFTER COMMENTARY
A brief pause between your commentary and the next move so the action stays readable. Less whiplash, more breathing room.

SETTINGS
New screen for managing your subscription (cancel or switch tiers without leaving the app), toggling the gold Studio home-screen icon on or off, and finding privacy and terms in one place.

LIBRARY POLISH
Long-press a video for a delete option with confirmation — in addition to the existing swipe-to-delete.

BUG FIXES
- Imports now handle chess.com PGN exports with blank Site/TimeControl tags.
- Clearer error when you paste an archived master-games URL.
- Video commentary now shows the animated waveform in sync with the audio.

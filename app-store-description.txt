Turn any chess game into a vertical short with cinematic sound, sliding pieces, capture cutscenes, face-cam or voice commentary, and AI-written captions — built for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

Tempo64 takes any PGN (or a chess.com link) and turns it into a 9:16 video for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts. Pieces slide with a thud, captures punch, checks exhale, checkmate rings out. Hook viewers with a 5-second climax teaser — a face-cam clip, the game's ending, or a cinematic cutscene that matches one of your captures. Talk over moves with face-cam or voice commentary, and let Apple Intelligence write your post caption — all on-device.

KEY FEATURES

- Paste any PGN — Chess.com, Lichess, or any standard export — or paste a chess.com URL and we fetch the moves
- Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920) MP4 ready for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube
- Cinematic capture cutscenes: short dramatic clips spliced in on qualifying captures, with their own audio and a film-style fade-in
- On-device AI heading and 2–3 sentence synopsis via Apple Intelligence (iPhone 15 Pro+, M-series iPad). Tap Regenerate to cycle angles, copy-paste with hashtags pre-baked.
- Engine-graded move quality and a live eval bar painted into the exported video — Great, Good, Mistake, Blunder, tied to the swing
- Brilliant/Blunder badges optional — keep the engine audio + eval bar without the over-board stickers
- Cartoon flame burst on the mating piece, with "nice" and "oof" stingers on Great and Blunder moves
- Animated piece slides on every move
- 5-second climax teaser: Auto, Game ending, any face-cam clip, or any cinematic cutscene your game qualifies for
- Library Regenerate — drop a saved game back into Review with one tap, re-render with different sounds, themes, or commentary
- Tap-to-trim: scrub to a position, tap Set start or Set end to anchor that ply
- Live render progress ring with a percentage readout
- Unified sound system: 10 sounds (Thud, Fahh, Deep Fahh, Bruh, Rizz, Chomp, Vine Boom, Gunshot, Plug, TikTok Core) across seven banks
- Three board themes, adjustable pace, and sound density
- Smart pacing tightens long quiet stretches without speeding up the dramatic moments
- Sounds never overlap into the next move, even at Extra Fast pace
- Background rendering — leave the app, get a notification when it's ready
- Save to Photos or share via the iOS share sheet

Turn any chess game into a vertical short with cinematic sound, sliding pieces, and voice or face-cam commentary — built for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Tempo64 takes the PGN of any chess game (or a chess.com link) and turns it into a 9:16 video. Every piece slides into place with a satisfying thud. Captures punch with a bite. Checks land with a dramatic "fahh." Checkmate rings out with a tail you'll want to share. Optional voice or face-cam commentary on any move you want to talk about.

KEY FEATURES

- Paste any PGN — works with Chess.com, Lichess, or any standard PGN export
- Paste a chess.com URL — Tempo64 fetches the moves automatically
- Vertical 9:16 (1080×1920) MP4 ready for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts
- Animated piece slides on every move so the action reads at any pace
- Cinematic sound design: 5 check sounds, 5 checkmate sounds, 3 capture sounds, plus thuds and attack stingers
- Three board themes: Classic, Lichess, Tournament
- File letters (a-h) and rank numbers (1-8) drawn on the board
- Adjustable pace: Slow, Normal, or Fast
- Live preview before render — tap or scrub through the game ply by ply
- Background rendering — leave the app, get a notification when it's ready
- Save to Photos or share via the iOS share sheet
- Your library of recent renders, with swipe-to-delete and long-press to remove

HOW IT WORKS

1. Paste a PGN or a chess.com URL (we show you how)
2. Review the parsed game with the built-in board scrubber
3. Customize theme, sounds, and pace; add voice or face-cam commentary on any move
4. Render, then save to Photos or share

PRIVACY

Tempo64 runs entirely on your device. Your PGN, your generated videos, your audio mix, your voice and face-cam recordings — everything stays local. We don't have a server. We don't collect your games. We don't track you.
